Real Name: Henrietta Peckington

Alias: Captain Cluck

Base of Operations: The Roost, deep beneath Clucksville Farm

Status: Missing in Action. Presumed legendary.

The Origin of a Hero

Captain Cluck wasn’t always the feathered force she became. Born the runt of her clutch on a quiet free-range farm, Henrietta was small, scrappy, and relentlessly curious. One fateful stormy night, after sheltering a brood of hatchlings from a downed power line, she was struck by a surge of pure energy—and transformed.

When she woke, she had the strength of ten roosters, the ability to glide short distances with astounding agility, and a mind sharper than any beak on the farm. From that day forward, Henrietta became something more.

She became Captain Cluck.

Powers & Abilities

Ultra Scratch Strength – Her claws can dig through solid concrete (and the toughest corn).

Feather Shield – With a flap of her wings, she creates a gust strong enough to deflect most projectiles.

The Peck of Justice – A high-speed, beak-first dive that sends villains flying.

Eggsplosive Orbs – Don’t ask how she lays them, just run.

Unbreakable Courage – When the coop’s in trouble, she never backs down.

A Legacy Etched in the Nest of Time

Captain Cluck’s heroics are known across barnyards and boardrooms alike. She ended the Great Fox War of '22, brought peace to the Poultry Union, and once stared down a hawk without blinking.

But her greatest act? Sacrificing her safety to save a transport truck full of orphaned chicks during the Grain Heist of Greendale. She disappeared into the clouds that day—last seen riding the tailwind of destiny.

“She wasn’t just a chicken. She was our chicken.”

The world may never see another like Captain Cluck. Though she’s gone, her wingspan stretches across our hearts.

May her spirit live on in every underdog… and every egg with potential.
